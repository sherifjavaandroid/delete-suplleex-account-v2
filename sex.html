To delete your account and all associated data from the Suplleex app:

  1. Open the app and log into your account.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account
  3. Confirm your deletion when prompted.

Your personal data such as username, email, and uploaded content will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.
